The Amazon Mechanical Turk (MTurk) is a crowdsourcing Internet marketplace that enables individuals or businesses (known as Requesters) to co-ordinate the use of human intelligence to perform tasks that computers are currently unable to do. The Requesters are able to post tasks known as HITs (Human Intelligence Tasks). Workers (more colloquially, Turkers) can then browse among existing tasks and complete them for a monetory payment set by the Requester. For more information please visit here

With Mkurk2.0, following new functionalities will be add:

  • LinkedIn Integration
  • BitCoin Payment Support
  • Smart Phone App
LinkedIn Integration
LinkedIn Integration will benefit both Workers and Requesters. Workers will be able to advertise their skill set endorsed by co-workers and peers in Mturk2.0 profile. Requesters will be able to select workers based on skills for their and confidently post higher level HITs.

BitCoin Payment
BitCoin is quickly becoming one of the leading currency alternatives and integrating it will greatly benifit Requesters and Workers.

Smart Phone App
A natve mobile app will provide full fledged functionalties for both Requesters and Workers. Requesters will be able to easily submit a new HIT, select the workers and make payment after receiving completed tasks. Workers will be able to search all HITs, submit the completed tasks and receive payments.

FAQ

How do I verify worker's skill set?
Amazon Mechanical Turk 2.0 is integrated with LinkedIn. This will give the workers chance to incorporate their professional skills in MTurk2.0 profile. In this way, requesters can easily assess worker's skills set endorsed by their co-workers and peers befor hiring them.
What are the payment options available in MTurk2.0?
Besides supporting all existing payment options, BitCoin is included as one of the payment option available to select.
Will there be a smart phone app?
MTurk2.0 will provide native mobile app that can be accessed by all leading smart pones OS. Both workers and requesters can perform their tasks via it.
